Cloudiness


The above cloudiness graph plots the cloud percentile based upon the latitude of the cities in the dataset. In this graph there are a couple things to note. The first is that this is January and you can see some tendency to be more cloudy in the northern hemishpere, most likely due to it being winter time. The second observation is more of a question on how cloudiness data is gathered as clearly there is some strata effects where distinct percentage lines (0%, 20%, 40%, ...) can be seen. This tends to indicate that collection methods favor setting observable clouds on some scale that picks the nearest major percent?
